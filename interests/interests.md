# Newsletter extraction focus

When processing newsletters, prioritise extracting:

## Facts to surface

- **New products/tools**: Launches, beta releases, significant updates to AI tools
- **Organisations**: New companies, research labs, or initiatives
- **Funding**: Investment rounds, grants, or major funding announcements
- **Research**: Papers, studies, or findings with practical implications
- **Events**: Conferences, deadlines, or significant dates

## Ideas to surface

- **Novel frameworks**: New ways of thinking about problems
- **Successful approaches**: Strategies or tactics that have worked for others
- **Contrarian takes**: Perspectives that challenge mainstream views
- **Predictions**: Forecasts about technology, markets, or society
- **Lessons learned**: Post-mortems, retrospectives, or hard-won insights

## Quotes worth noting

- Quotable insights from notable figures
- Concise formulations of important ideas
- Surprising statistics or data points

## Action item triggers

Flag content that suggests potential actions:
- Tools worth trying
- People worth following or reaching out to
- Events worth attending
- Ideas to incorporate into current work
